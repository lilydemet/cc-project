here is where i draft and synthesize my fieldnotes. because my toolkit contains an eclectic set of methods and technologies, my initial (raw) data is spread across multiple devices and file formats. in this space i juxtapose ideas and let them simmer. entries come in no particular order. maybe when i figure out how to build this page nonlinearly i can better convey their inter-relation. like the thoughts in my mind, nothing here is self-contained. rather, i'm constantly building, iterating, synthesizing, moving on, and returning, to places/practices/concepts/theories. Write Iris van der Tuin and Nanna Verhoeff (2022, 2-3):

The creative aspect in creative humanities takes shape in the (literally) productive connection between making practices and thinking practices: making as/through thinking and thinking as/through making. These generative practices are emphatically experimental and comfortable with knowledge production in uncertainty, multiplicity, and friction. Thinking with, through, and beyond concepts, creative humanities projects are creative in designing and developing their own methods and approaches as they seek to navigate and explore the productive connections and reciprocal relationships between the creative practices they engage with and to develop conceptual analytical approaches for what these practices also work with, through, or beyond.
here is where i offer insight into my process of thinking/making into being various projects in the creative humanities. right now my focus is on deep mapping and the making of a lost poem of vancouver. this page is in evolution. its unfinished and uncertain. a collage, my field notes constitute themselves a field from which i draw out the basis for some construction.
